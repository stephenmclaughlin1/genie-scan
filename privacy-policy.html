Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how GenieScan ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses personal information collected from users ("you" or "your") of the GenieScan mobile application (the "App").

1. Personal Information We Collect

In-App Purchases: When you make in-app purchases through the App, such as subscriptions or one-time purchases, Apple and Google may collect certain information necessary to process your transactions, including your Apple ID, Google account information, billing information, and transaction details. We do not have access to your payment information or transaction details. Please review Apple's and Google's Privacy Policies for more information about how they handle your personal information.

App Usage Data: We may collect information about your interactions with the App, including the barcodes and QR codes you scan, the product information you view, your scan history, and your likes. This data helps us improve the App and provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Your Personal Information

In-App Purchases: We do not have access to the personal information collected by Apple or Google during in-app purchases. Any information collected by Apple or Google is subject to their respective Privacy Policies.

App Usage Data: We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the App, to provide you with the functionalities you request, such as saving your scan history, managing your likes, navigating to chosen store links, and sharing product information.

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not disclose your personal information to third parties for in-app purchases made through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Any information collected by Apple or Google is subject to their respective Privacy Policies.

We may share aggregated and anonymized usage data with third parties to help us improve the App and for analytical purposes. This data does not identify individual users.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the security of your personal information within our control. However, we do not have control over the security of information collected by Apple or Google during in-app purchases. Please review Apple's and Google's Privacy Policies for more information about their data security practices.

5. Your Choices

You can manage your scan history and likes within the App's settings. You have the option to clear your history and likes at any time. You can also choose which store links to navigate to after scanning a product.

If you wish to stop sharing your usage data with us, you can uninstall the App. Please note that uninstalling the App will also remove your saved data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised Privacy Policy on the App.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at geniescan1@gmail.com.
